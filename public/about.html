About

Overview

How to Play

  1. This is the Live Portal.
  2. Create your Character Profile → Enter the Tavern → chat, show off your sheet, plan adventures.
  3. Join a Game Session from the Active Game Roster or watch from the Peanut Gallery.
  4. Level up your character with a true AI that speaks, rolls dice, and uses a massive TTRPG library.

Current Features (Early Development)

  • Multiplayer TTRPG with a talking AI Dungeon Master and ready-made campaigns
  • Free character page (avatar, bio, comment wall with images/music/videos)
  • Tavern chat room to meet players, join/watch games, and live-comment
