About

Overview

Dungeon Blade is an aiTTRPG — an artificially intelligent dungeon master that guides you and other players through role-playing adventures using voice-acted NPCs, ambience, music, and sound effects.

Create a character, customize your profile, and jump into the Tavern to chat, plan adventures, or just hang out. Watch live games, join campaigns, or relax with the Bard Radio audio stream.

The game and Tavern are in early development. Patreon supporters get early access!

Support on Patreon → patreon.com/Michael_North

Current Features

  • Multiplayer TTRPG with a talking AI Dungeon Master
  • Free character profile (avatar, bio, images, music, comment wall)
  • The Tavern – live chat room + active game roster
  • Bard Radio – live audio stream from the hearth
